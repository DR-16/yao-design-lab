01

Turning Ideas Into Things

把想法变成事物

Most of my projects begin as simple thoughts. Building them helps me understand what works, what fails.

我的大多数项目，都从一个简单的想法开始。把它们做出来，才能看清什么行得通、什么不行。

02

Curiosity Has Always Been My Motivation

好奇心一直是我的动力

Business, technology, design, and psychology are the areas of mine. Exploring different areas often leads to unexpected connections and new possibilities.

商业、技术、设计与心理学，是我关切的几个领域。在它们之间穿行，常常带来意想不到的连接和崭新的可能。

03

Creation Is How I Learn

创造是我学习的方式

I was born in 2009. Since the age of fifteen, creating value has become one of the ways I define myself.

我出生于 2009 年。从十五岁起，创造价值成为我定义自己的方式之一。

“Don’t Ever Leave Yourself Behind Today”
