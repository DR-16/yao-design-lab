v1 · 起手式opening move

陪伴 = 记忆 + 关系。一个见面就喊"老公"的 AI 我自己都受不了。 Companionship = memory + distance. An AI that calls you "babe" on day one isn't a relationship.

v2 · 第一次大转向first pivot

她答得越好, 我越空。永远友好是 ChatGPT 的礼貌训练 — 不是关系。 The better she answered, the emptier I felt. Always-polite is RLHF — not relationship.

v2.2 · 一行代码one-line pivot

真人发微信, 是一句一句发的, 不是写完一段才发。 Real people text sentence by sentence, not in finished paragraphs.

v3 · 第二次大转向second pivot

决定不把它做成生意。"不上传"要写进架构, 不是写在 README。 Decided not to commercialise. "No upload" belongs in the architecture, not the README.

v4 · 第三次大转向third pivot

中文她叫小爱, 英文她叫什么。i18n 不是翻译, 是身份。 In Chinese she's 小爱 — what's her name in English. i18n isn't translation, it's identity.

尾声 · 现在回头看 EPILOGUE · LOOKING BACK

这 6 个月
学到的几件事 A few things learned
over six months

01

技术不是瓶颈了, 设计才是。 Tech isn't the bottleneck. Design is.

本地 LLM + 三层记忆, 真的能跑出一个有人味的 AI。瓶颈在于愿不愿意接受她"不够友好"。 Local LLM + three-layer memory is enough to ship something human. The bottleneck is whether you accept her being "not always nice."

02

"不完美"才是产品的灵魂。 Imperfection is the soul of it.

永远友好的 AI 不像人 — 像客服。情绪/冷战/记仇是让"陪伴"可信的最低门槛。 A perpetually nice AI reads as customer-service, not as a person. Moods are the minimum threshold for "companionship" to feel real.

03

开源是 principle, 不是 marketing。 Open source as principle, not marketing.

技术约束比 brand promise 可靠。我宁愿放弃一个商业模式, 也不愿对自己说谎。 An architectural constraint outranks a brand promise. I'd rather drop a revenue model than lie to myself.

04

这不是为了做 AI 女友。 It wasn't really about an AI girlfriend.

是用"陪伴"这个最日常的关系, 反过来探测"人"是怎么被感知的。 It used "companionship" — the most everyday relationship — to probe how we perceive personhood at all.

下一步 · 还没动手, 但想了很久 NEXT · not built yet, but planned

  • 记忆从 ChromaDB 换成 graph DB — 关系是网状的, 不是平的。Move memory from ChromaDB to a graph DB — relationships are networked, not flat.
  • 情绪学习曲线 — 她冷淡之后怎么和好, 应该有节奏。Emotion learning curve — the return from cold to warm needs rhythm.
  • 心情时间线 UI — 用户能看见她最近一周的情绪走势。Mood timeline UI — let the user see her past week.
  • 真正的 user study — 5 个陌生人用 30 天。A real user study — five strangers for 30 days.
