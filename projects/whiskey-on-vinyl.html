A room, not a bar.

我想做的不是另一家酒吧。是想看 — 在所有都要快、要翻台、要 KPI 的城市里, 一个不催促人的房间, 还能不能存在。 I didn't want to design another bar. I wanted to see — in a city where everything wants you to be faster, in and out, hitting KPIs — whether a room that doesn't rush you could still exist.

v1 → v2 · 第一次转向first pivot

从"我们卖什么"
到"为什么应该来" From "what we sell"
to "why you'd come"

v1 是标准独立酒吧 landing page — 主视觉、菜单、地址、预约表单。做完看了一眼关掉, 一周没再打开。 v1 was the standard indie-bar playbook — hero visual, menu, address, reservation form. I finished it, looked at it once, closed it, didn't reopen for a week.

问题不是它不够好看 — 是它在解释"我们卖什么", 完全没回答"为什么应该来"。v2 之后整个项目转向 attitude 的设计: 不卖 inventory, 卖一种允许。 The problem wasn't that it looked bad — it explained what we sell without ever answering why you'd come. After v2, the whole project pivoted to designing an attitude: not selling inventory, but selling a kind of permission.

The first draft sold drinks.
This one sells permission.

v2.2 · 一根针, 走了三版one hand, three drafts

把黑胶
变成一只钟 Turning vinyl
into a clock

v2.1 给首屏的黑胶加上真实时间的指针。一开始时针是普通调酒长匙 — 视觉上"太硬", 像工具不像氛围。 v2.1 gave the hero vinyl real clock hands. The hour hand started as a regular bar spoon — "too hard," more tool than atmosphere.

换成苦艾酒匙 (镂空叶片 + 短柄) 之后, 时针有了仪式感; 分针换成水果鸡尾酒签 + 黑橄榄。一根针走三版, 才像。 Swapped to an absinthe spoon (slotted leaf + short shaft) and the hour hand gained ritual; the minute became a cocktail pick + black olive. One hand, three drafts before it felt right.

这一段没什么大故事 — 但它是整个 v2 网站里我最满意的一个细节。 No big narrative here — but it's the detail in v2 I'm proudest of.

v2.1.1 → v2.2.1 · bar spoon → absinthe → pick

v3 · 在没有房子之前before the room exists

先把光
画出来 Paint the light
first

v3 开始想做空间。没实体场地, 所以先在脑子里走一遍 — 入口缓冲、酒柜+音乐总控、主厅、VIP、调酒区、卫生间。所有的尺度、色温、声学吸音参数 (RT60 ≈ 0.4-0.6s) 都先在一张平面图上对齐。 v3 turned to space. With no physical site, I had to walk the room in my head first — entrance buffer, cellar + music control, main hall, VIP, bar, restrooms. All scale, colour-temp, acoustic absorption (RT60 ≈ 0.4-0.6s) aligned on one floorplan before anything else.

然后用 FLUX.1-merged 生成了 20 张实景渲染 — 9pm 2700K → 12am 2200K → 2am 1800K, 整间屋子按时间慢慢沉进夜里。 Then FLUX.1-merged generated 20 photoreal renders — 9pm 2700K → 12am 2200K → 2am 1800K. The whole room slowly settles into the night.

v3.5 · 工程含量最高的一段the engineering moment

137 MB → 3.49 MB 137 MB → 3.49 MB

用 Microsoft TRELLIS 把所有 AI 实景图反过来 multidiffusion 融成一个完整的 GLB 场景。原始模型 137 MB, Safari 加载到一半就崩。 Microsoft TRELLIS fed all the AI scenes back in and multidiffusion-fused them into one complete GLB. Raw model: 137 MB — Safari crashed mid-load.

用 gltf-transform 加 Draco 几何压缩 — 3.49 MB, 网页可以即时加载。这一段是整个项目里, 我自己学到最多的部分。 After gltf-transform + Draco geometry compression — 3.49 MB, instant in-browser load. The most I learned in the whole project came out of this segment.

尝试过的工作链: Three.js 手搭 / Spline 导出 / Hunyuan3D 单件 — 全失败。TRELLIS 是第一个跑通的。 Earlier attempts: hand-built Three.js / Spline export / Hunyuan3D single objects — all failed. TRELLIS was the first that ran through.

TRELLIS · multidiffusion · Draco geometry

v4 · 选择不做choosing not to make more

3D 暂停 —
等真实的房子 3D paused —
waiting for the real room

v3.5 之后, 我把 3D 模块封存了。原因: 这间屋子终究是要建出来的, 一旦实体存在, 用 iPhone LiDAR 扫一次就能得到 100% 真实的模型。任何"想象的渲染"都会被替代。 After v3.5, I shelved the 3D module. The reason — this room will eventually be built, and once a physical space exists, one iPhone LiDAR scan gets you a 100% truthful model. Any "imagined render" gets overwritten.

继续在没有实体的阶段堆 AI 模型, 反而是在欺骗访客"你看到的就是它的样子"。事实是: 还没有人看到。 Stacking more AI without a real space is lying to visitors — "this is what it looks like." Truth: no one has seen it yet.

The discipline isn't making more.
It's knowing when to stop.
v3.5 → v4.0 · stop · wait for LiDAR

01

一个网站的真正问题是: 它在回答什么。 A site's real question is: what is it answering?

v1 解释"我们卖什么"。v2 改成回答"为什么应该来"。同一份内容, 不同的问题, 站点的灵魂就完全不一样。 v1 explained "what we sell." v2 answered "why you'd come." Same content, different question, completely different soul.

02

AI 渲染是脚手架, 不是建筑。 AI renders are scaffolding, not architecture.

20 张实景图 + GLB 模型让我在没建之前先把屋子走完一遍。但它们不该被当成成品交付 — 一旦实体存在, 它们的功能就完成了。 Twenty renders + one GLB let me walk the room before it was built. But they shouldn't be shipped as the final product — once the physical space exists, their job is done.

03

知道什么时候停, 比做更多更难。 Knowing when to stop is harder than making more.

v3.5 之后再加东西都是反向的 — 让项目"看起来更厉害", 但离它本来要回答的问题更远。v4 决定停下, 是整个项目里我最难做的决定。 After v3.5 every addition would have run backwards — making the project look more impressive while pulling it further from the question it was meant to answer. Choosing to stop at v4 was the hardest decision in the whole project.

04

项目最终的形状, 是知道"再做下去就在撒谎"。 The project's final shape is recognising when further work would be a lie.

这间屋子还没建。所有的渲染、3D、菜单, 都是想象。把它做得"像完成品"就是在撒谎 — 我宁愿这页保持"未完待续"。 The room isn't built. Every render, 3D scene, menu is imagined. Polishing it to "look finished" would be a lie — I'd rather this page stay openly unfinished.
